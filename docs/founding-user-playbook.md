# Founding User Playbook

## Goal

Turn passive interest into a small queue of real people you can onboard personally.

## Current Product Support

The product now includes a simple founding-user waitlist flow backed by the database.

Use it to collect:

- name
- email
- segment
- short context on how they would use Followup

## Who To Target First

- freelancers
- tiny agencies
- consultants

## What To Offer

- early staging access
- founding-user pricing
- direct onboarding help
- the chance to shape the workflow before broader launch

## Immediate Next Steps

1. add 10 real prospects to the queue
2. talk to them manually
3. watch where setup breaks
4. convert the best-fit users into trial or paid accounts
